About this model
This prototype uses a deep CNN-based pipeline. In Breed mode, it uses an EfficientNet-style backbone trained on Ayrshire, Brown Swiss, Holstein, Jersey, and Red Dane. In Disease mode, it uses a TensorFlow CNN trained to detect Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD), Pinkeye (IBK), and Lumpy Skin Disease (LSD).
